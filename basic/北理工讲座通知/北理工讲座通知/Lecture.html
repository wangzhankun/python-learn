    报告人：Zheng-Dong Ma（Mechanical Engineering, University of Michigan）
    报告时间：2018年12月4日15:00-16:30
    报告地点：车重实验楼二楼学术报告厅
    报告语言：中文/英文
    报告内容：With the technical progresses, architectured materials are possible to be designed and manufactured with consideration of structure functionality and performance requirements. Using combination of multiple materials, macro-architectured materials could adaptively strengthen cellular structures, leading to light-weight and high-performance designs. This seminar covers the following topics:
    (1) 微结构材料的基本概念和主要特点;
    (2) 数字化设计和多尺度分析方法;
    (3) 功能导向设计和优化方法；
    (4) 数字化产品和物理产品实例；
    (5) 微结构材料的产业化应用前景；

Zheng-Dong Ma has done absolutely excellent job in his research. His major findings are summarized in 59 articles in refereed journals and transactions, 60 articles in refereed conference and symposium proceedings, 13 refereed conference abstracts, 41 non-refereed conference abstracts, and 1 book chapter.     题目：Unveiling and controlling ultrafast electronic properties in solids
    报告人：刘海云 副研究员 （北京工业大学 激光工程研究院）
    时 间：2018年11月24日(周六)下午 3:30
    地 点：北京理工大学中心教学楼501

    摘要：
    Stimulation of electronic systems in solids under the irradiation of intense and ultrashort laser pulses with femtosecond pulse duration, is a unique way of studying and controlling novel non-equilibrium states that are beyond static ground states, for example, light-induced insulator-to-metal transition and light-induced superconductivity, etc. In this talk, I will first briefly introduce the development of a time- and angle-resolved photoemission spectroscopy (TR-ARPES) based on high-order harmonic generation (HHG), and its application for light-induced insulator-to-metal transition and detection of collective modes. Then I will focus on the ultrafast optical response of THz and Near-IR, and our recent findings on transient states in semiconductors, cuprate superconductors and topological matters.
